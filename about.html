I’m Sara, a graphic design artist located in Manhattan, N.Y, studying at Parsons School of Design for Communication Design. Welcome to my portfolio website, that I coded 100% from scratch.*

I have proficient skills in Adobe Creative Cloud applications and Figma, and intermediate skills in various coding platforms with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

My journey in graphic design begins from a very young age. I have taken classes and done extracurriculars in both coding and fine arts since elementary and middle school. Then, in high school, I began experimenting with the intersectionality of technology and art, which led me to realizing that I want to pursue a career in graphic design.
